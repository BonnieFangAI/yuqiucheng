关于 About

艺术家、精神分析师，现居广州。七岁师从国画家邓少峰，习画六年；1997年师从湖北美院油画系主任胡朝阳；2005年后追随尚扬先生。

他在工厂废弃纸板上的偶发图像中发现绘画性，将铁丝网架、塑料残片、有机玻璃等工业现成物重新组合为装置绘画——他称之为「现成品的二度绘画性」。近年将实践延伸至广州白云心理医院，以拉康式精神分析介入社会现场，倾听那些被排斥的声音。

Artist and psychoanalyst based in Guangzhou. He studied traditional Chinese painting under Deng Shaofeng from age seven; trained under Hu Chaoyang, chair of Oil Painting at the Hubei Institute of Fine Arts; and followed the mentorship of Shang Yang from 2005.

He discovers painterly qualities in the incidental images on discarded factory cardboard, recombining wire mesh, plastic fragments, and plexiglass into installation paintings — what he calls "the secondary painterliness of readymades." In recent years he has extended his practice to Guangzhou's Baiyun Psychiatric Hospital, intervening in social reality through Lacanian psychoanalysis, listening to the voices that society excludes.

实践 Practice

精神分析

在广州白云心理医院设立工作室，充当住院者的「耳朵」。在讲述与聆听之间建立移情关系——他从拉康那里学到：不可思议的移情本身就是一种屋漏痕，也是艺术。

Psychoanalysis

Working from a studio at Baiyun Psychiatric Hospital, he becomes the ear for those society excludes. Between telling and listening, transference emerges — from Lacan he learned that this transference itself is a trace, and therefore art.

艺术治疗

在癌症康复营开展绘画治疗，融合侘寂美学与佛教观想。引导参与者将毒素释放于纸上，再撕裂，再重组。侘——贫穷、无常。寂——孤独、寂静。不完美才是另一种完美。

Art Therapy

Leading painting therapy in cancer recovery programs, he merges wabi-sabi with Buddhist visualization. Participants release toxins onto paper — then tear, then reassemble. Wabi: poverty, impermanence. Sabi: solitude, stillness. Imperfection as the only perfection.

当我们讨论艺术的时候，反而是要清除艺术。每个人的生命都是艺术。

When we discuss art, what we really need is to strip art away. Every life is art.

— 余秋呈
